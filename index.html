WHO ARE WE

Hello, we are MIKE and MINDY. We are now in the process of divesting from a sustainable farming family business.
We have started a nonprofit organization (NPO) to help combat food waste created by the local major supermarkets and distributors in their area.

OUR MISSION

"Pay As You Feel"
We divert food waste into a sustainable restaurant situation where people can pay if they can, and if they are unable, they can still get a meal of nourishing food for themselves and their family.
Our goal is to bring nourishing food to struggling individuals and families while preserving dignity and autonomy for the individual.
Let's Join Us

HOW WE OPERATE

Working with the local supermarkets and distributors, we will make a changeable daily menu using diverted fresh food and other foodstuffs that would otherwise go to the landfill.
We have started a “pay as you feel” food box that is full of fresh, diverted food from the grocery stores and farmers markets available from select pick-up locations within the city on certain days.
